Plugins

OpenVeo works with plugins to extend its features.

Official plugins

Official plugins are plugins developped and maintained by the OpenVeo team. They all are available on NPM OpenVeo scope.

Unofficial plugins

Unofficial plugins developped by the community must be prefixed by openveo- and distributed under the AGPL-3.0 license.
